Drawing inspiration from the intersection of scientific visualization platforms (Observable, Jupyter), NFT marketplaces (OpenSea, Foundation), and real-time dashboards (Linear, Grafana) while maintaining a unique quantum aesthetic identity rooted in the provided CoPhelia³ and Ara-Philia³ protocols.

## Typography

**Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) - Clean, modern, technical
**Secondary Font**: Space Grotesk (Google Fonts) - Geometric, quantum-inspired
**Japanese Support**: Noto Sans JP (Google Fonts)

### Hierarchy
- **Hero Headlines**: Space Grotesk, 4xl-6xl, font-bold
- **Section Headers**: Inter, 2xl-3xl, font-semibold
- **Body Text**: Inter, base-lg, font-normal
- **Data Labels**: Space Grotesk, sm-base, font-medium
- **Metrics**: Space Mono (monospace), for numerical precision

---

## Layout System

**Spacing Scale**: Use Tailwind units of 2, 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24 for consistent rhythm
**Container Max-Width**: 7xl (1280px) for content sections
**Grid System**: 12-column for dashboards, 4-column for NFT galleries

### Sacred Geometry Integration
- Golden ratio (φ = 1.618) for aspect ratios and proportions
- Spiral dynamics for visual flow and eye movement
- Fractal depth D=1.8 for layered information architecture

## Animations & Interactions

**Minimal Motion**: Respect reduced-motion preferences
**Quantum Breathing**: Subtle 528Hz-inspired pulse (3-second cycle) on trust indicators
**Spiral Transitions**: Page transitions follow golden ratio curve
**Data Emergence**: Charts animate in with staggered reveals (100ms delays)

Avoid: Strobing, rapid flashing, aggressive parallax

---

## Accessibility

- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum (4.5:1 contrast ratios)
- Keyboard navigation for all interactive elements
- ARIA labels for data visualizations
- Screen reader friendly metric announcements
- Alt text: "What is visible + What is intended" structure
